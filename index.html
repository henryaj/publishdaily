The Challenge

Like Inkhaven but online. It's designed for writers who want to build a consistent publishing habit through community support and meaningful commitment.

Commitment: Publish 500+ words every day for 30 days in November 2025

Investment: Single buy-in ($25 USD) into a shared tontine pot

Reward: Complete the challenge and split the pot with other finishers

Community: Daily support and accountability through our Slack community

Challenge Rules

Publish every day

Write and publish 500+ words every single day. No exceptions, no catch-up days.

Write in public

Publish anywhere public: personal blog, Medium, Substack, or your preferred platform.

Midnight PST deadline

Posts must be published before midnight Pacific time each day.

Original content only

All writing must be original. No republishing or recycling existing content.

Miss a day and you're out of the tontine (but welcome to continue for community support).
