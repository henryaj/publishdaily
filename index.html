Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to the money?

All buy-in amounts go into a shared pot. Everyone who completes the full 30-day challenge splits the pot equally. If you miss a day, you forfeit your share, but you're welcome to continue writing for community support.

What if I'm new to writing?

Writers of all experience levels are welcome! The 500-word minimum is deliberately achievable while still being meaningful. Our community includes beginners through experienced writers.

Can I write about anything?

Yes! Fiction, non-fiction, personal essays, technical writing, poetry - any genre is welcome as long as it meets the word count and originality requirements.

How is this different from NaNoWriMo?

Unlike NaNoWriMo's private writing approach, PublishDaily focuses on daily publishing and community accountability. The financial commitment and public nature create different incentives for consistency and quality.

What platform should I use?

Any public platform works: personal blog, Medium, Substack, LinkedIn articles, even Twitter threads (if they meet word count). The key is that your writing must be publicly accessible.
